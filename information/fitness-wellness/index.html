Information about the fitness and wellness space

On board of this space have a fitness and wellness space to keep your body in shape during your trip to mars.
The fitness and wellness space can be found at the back side of the spaceship.


Equipment in the fitness and wellness space

Treadmill: With magnetic resistance, designed for use in microgravity with harnesses to keep you secured while running.
Cycle Ergometer: A stationary bike that works in low gravity conditions.
Rowing Machine with Flywheel Resistance: Modified for zero-gravity workouts.
Extra heavy weights: Designed for use in low gravity conditions.
Stabilized Yoga Mat: Equipped with straps to hold poses during low-gravity yoga sessions.
Infrared Sauna Pod: Compact relaxation pod for muscle recovery and wellness.
Hydromassage Bed: Offers water-based massages to alleviate tension and stress.
Oxygen Therapy Station: Provides high-purity oxygen for post-workout recovery.
Meditation Booth: Equipped with guided meditations and soothing ambient sounds. And noise-cancelling features.
Resistance Bands: Versatile bands for strength training and flexibility exercises.
Space Pilates Reformer: Adapted for low-gravity conditions to enhance core strength and flexibility.
Elliptical Trainer: Designed for cardiovascular workouts in microgravity.
Balance Trainer: Helps improve stability and coordination in low-gravity environments.
Foam Roller: For muscle recovery and myofascial release.
